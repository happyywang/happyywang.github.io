David Perell 50天写作课程（七）:学会快速写作

David Perell 50天写作课程（七）:学会快速写作 Learn to write fast

拥有笔记系统，写作就会变得容易很多。作家David Allen说：Minute to minute and day to day you do not have time to think. You need to have already thought. 建立第二大脑，即建立自己的笔记系统是复用自己已有想法的最好方式。Instead of writing from scratch, you can create an outline by collecting your most relevant ideas from books you have already read, conversations you have already had and evergreen notes you have already written. 从这些Ideas中先搭建出一个文章的框架。

说话也会迫使大脑来快速组织想法。所以设置一个三分钟的计数器，假装在和一个朋友交谈，给他解释你想写的内容，然后用Otter软件将对话转化成文字。

不是每篇文章都要速写，但是just as drills can build an athlete’s skill set, every writer should practice writing fast.

Writing fast is thinking fast, 所以强制自己build a note taking system and structure ideas by talking
