来自James Clear的一段分享

周末啦，过了高效的一周，很满，很累。今天只想分享一段话，来自James Clear (原子习惯的作者)

“A sure sign you have not done enough reading is if you find yourself agreeing with whatever book you read last.

At first, it is easy to be swayed by any reasonable argument. Once you have read a lot, you can see that even the best arguments have limitations.”

所以周末没事多读读书吧。
