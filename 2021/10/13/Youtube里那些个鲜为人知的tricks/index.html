YouTube里那些个鲜为人知的tricks

YouTube里那些个鲜为人知的tricks (@Harsh Makadia)：

  1. 创建从某个时间点开始的视频链接 Create links that start at specific times.

    How?

    • Click shar on any video
    • Check the start at the box
    • Adjust the time
    • Copy the link

  2. 安装广告拦截器 YouTube Ad blocker

  3. 关于如何从YouTube视频创建Gif

    How?

    • Open the video
    • Add the word “gif” before YouTube in the URL
    • Customize your GIF

  4. 关于如何下载YouTube视频

    How?

    • Open the video
    • Add the word “ss” before YouTube in the URL
    • Download the video

  5. 使用键盘快捷键来导航视频 Navigate YouTube with keyboard shortcuts

    常用快捷键：

    k: Pause/Play in player

    m: Mute/unmute the video

    j: seek backward 10 seconds in player

    l: seek forward 10 seconds in player

    Home/End on the seek bar: seek to the beginning/last seconds of the video.

    Up/Down arrow on the seek bar: Increase/Decrease volume 5%

    Number 1 to 9 on the seek bar: seek to the 10% to 90% of the video.

    Number 0 on the seek bar: seek to the beginning of the video

    f: active full screen or exit to full screen

    c: activate subtitles or hide again

    Shift + N: move to the next video

    Shift + P: move to the previous video.

  6. Enable picuture in picture

    需要安装Picuture in picuture 扩展工具。

安装了Ad blocker，世界清净多了。
