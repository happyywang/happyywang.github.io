David-Perell-50天写作课程（二十二）write about earned secrets

Earned secrets是一些只有你才写得出的ideas, 是一些你通过环境或努力工作收获的。Peter Thiel在Zero to one一书中写道：The big secret is that there are many secrets left to uncover. There are still many large white spaces on the map of human knowledge. You can go discover them. So do it. Get out there and fill in the vlank spaces. Every single moment is a possibility to go to these new places and explore them.

Earned secrets来自两方面：access and revelation.

Access来自一种独特的视角。it happens 当你拥有独家信息，这种信息更够产生以前从未被写过的ideas.

Revelation happens when you synthesize public information in new ways, either because of obsessive research or a unique eye. 媒体只能覆盖世界各地发生的部分事件，你可以通过另外的视角去探寻那些被人遗忘的故事。而独特的视角通常是专业的副产品A unique eye is usually a byproduct of experise.

Access and revelation的秘密可能是被隐藏的，但他们是存在的。作家的任务就是发现它，然后分享出来。
