David-Perell-50天写作课程（二十一）假定你并非原创 Assume you are not original

Derek Sivers曾写过一篇文章：Assume you are below average。大部分人都觉得自己在平均水平之上，Silver恰恰相反，他说假定自己在平均水平之下encourages him to ask questions and listen more. 他说，假定自己在平均水平之下就是承认自己还需要学习，需要提高而不是自满。

无独有偶，网上还有一篇文章：Assume you are not original. 你怎么也无法产生新ideas，怎么办？心理学家Rene Girard争辩说人类本身就是天然的巨型模仿机器。哈佛人类学家Joseph Henrich甚至说模仿能力是我们成功的秘诀所在。The urge to copy is in our DNA.

那么没有原创能力的人如何获取unique ideas?

Use the internet to curate your intellectual environment so you can copy the silver of people who do not copy people.

传统社会奖励那些Fit in的人，而互联网奖励有着vivid personal monopolies的杰出者。,rather than trying to find original ideas on your own, surround yourself with people who are missing the imitation gene and have a genetic disposition for originality. then build upon their best ideas.
