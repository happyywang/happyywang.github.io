David-Perell-50天写作课程（四十五）成为小众一族 Get Wonky

Patrick O’Shaughness运作着一档世界上最大的投资类播客Invest like the best，同时拥有着一群忠实的听众,甚至包括spotify 的CEO Daniel Ek 等。听众表示喜欢源自它的独一无二性：there is nothing else like it on the internet.

Patrick表示：On the internet, the more focused you are, the more wonky and niche you are, the bigger your audience is. The internet rewards the edges of distributions.

所以尽量让自己成为小众一族，去吸引和自己志同道合的那个小圈子。Once you get wonky, you will have a combination of ideas nobody is ever seen before.
