来自Noah Kagan的一条建议

最近在读巨人的工具，每天会从书中挑一两个感兴趣的人去听听他们的人生建议。今天选了Noah Kagan. 他曾经是Facebook的第30号员工，Mint.com的第四号员工，SumoMe的创始人。

印象最深的是这句：

Do not try and find time. Schedule Time

书中说：On Tuesdays from 10 a.m. to 12 noon, Noah schedules nothing but “learning”. This is a great reminder that, for anything important, you do not find time. It is only real if it is on the calendar.
