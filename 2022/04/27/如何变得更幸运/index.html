如何变得更幸运

如何能变得更幸运，可以试试下面的方法

  • Get in the Arena

    There is no luck on the sidelines, the arena is where it strikes. It is lonely and vulnerable, but it is the only path that bears real fruit. When faced with 2 paths, choose the path that puts you in the arena-where collisions happen, where luck can strike.

  • Be proactive

    Take responsibility for your life. By being proactive, instead of reactive. You stop the blame and you reclaim your own power to change your life.

  • Practice gratitude

  • Surround yourself with the right people

  • Be clear about your goals and voice them

  • Send more cold Emails

  • Schedule free time

    when you have free time, you have the headspace to pursue new ideas and go down rabbit holes. Plan for more of it.

  • Create value without expectations

  • Positive thinking attracts luck

  • Hang out with optimists and ruthlessly eliminate negativity

  • Work harder, be luckier

  • Increase your Karma, get luckier

  • Become an expert

  • Create Boredom

    Some of your most creative moments come during periods of boredom. You are bored, your mind wonders, your thoughts mingle. Bam, insight strikes.

  • Ask great cold questions

  • Follow your curiosity
