Naval'laws

Naval’laws:

  • Be present above all else
  • Desire is suffering (Desire is a contract you make with yourself to be unhappy until you get what you want)
  • Anger is a hot coal that you hold in your hand while waiting to throw it at someone else
  • If you can not see yourself working with someone for life, do not work with them for a day
  • Reading(learning) is the ultimate meta-skill and can be traded for anything else
  • All the real benefits in life come from compound interest.
  • Earn with your mind, not your time
  • Total honestry at all times, it is almost always possible to be honest and positive
  • Praise specifically, criticise generally
  • Watch every thought (always asks, why am I having this thought)
  • Love is given, not received
  • Enlightenment is the space between your thoughts
  • Mathematics is the language of nature
  • Every moment has to be complete n and of itself.
