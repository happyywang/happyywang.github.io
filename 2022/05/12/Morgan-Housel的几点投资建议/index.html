Morgan Housel的投资建议

Morgan Housel的投资建议：

If you want to do better as an interstor, the single most powerful thing you can do is increase your time horizon. Time is the most powerful force in investing. It makes little things grow big and big mistakes fade away. it can not neutralize luck and risk, but it pushes results closer towards what people deserve.

Become OK with a lot of things going wrong. You can be wrong half the time and still make a fortune, because a small minority of things account for the majority of outcomes.

Use money to gain control over your time, because not having control your time is such a powerful and universal drag on happiness. The ability to do what you want, when you want, with who you want, for as long as you want to, pays the highest dividend that exits in finance.

Be nicer and less flashy.

Save, just save. saving for things that are impossible to predict or define is one of the best reason to save.

Nothing worthwhile is free. Uncertainty, doubt and regret are common costs in the finance world. They are often worth paying. but you have to view them as fees rather than fines.
