有关遗憾

通常我们都认为: 遗憾是自我否定的(self-defeating)，向后看的(backward-looking)，是一种要不惜一切代价避免的消极感觉 a negative feeling to avoid at all costs.

然而，Yugeta通过她的故事却告诉我们遗憾也可以成为助力（propellant）。63岁的Yugeta全程马拉松跑进3小时，打破了自己之前的创造的60岁以上年龄段的世界纪录。在她50多岁开始训练前，只是一个普通的妇女，有着四个孩子，在中学教物理。驱动她跑马拉松的动力源自学生时代落选参加奥运会资格的遗憾。她说：

I do not think the feeling of regret is a negative emotion. What is negative are thoughts like: I can not run fast anymore or I am too old to do this. and I think that it is an entirely positive way to live, to use any regrets you might have as motivation to achieve a goal.

It is a waste of time to think about days gone by. what is important is the here and now. and the future. How can you improve yourself in the days to come
