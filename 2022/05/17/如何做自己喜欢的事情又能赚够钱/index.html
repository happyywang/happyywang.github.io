如何做自己喜欢的事情又能赚够钱

关于如何做自己喜欢的事情又能赚够钱(How to do what you love and make good money), Derek Sivers给出下面的建议：

  • Have a well-paying job
  • Seriously pursue your passion for love, not money

他说需要做好两者的平衡，We all have a need for stability and adventure, certainty and uncertainty, money and passion.

Do something for love and something for money. Do not try to make one thing satisfy your entire life.

Each half of your life becomes a remedy for the other.

About the job: be smart, build up the rare skills that are well-rewarded.

About the passion: pursue it seriously, take lessons, make weekly progress, keep improving.

The main obstacle to this amazing life will be self-control. you will need good time management to stop addictions like social media and video-watching, and make your passion your main relaxing activity.

Do not try to make your job your whole life. and do not try to make your passion your sole income.
