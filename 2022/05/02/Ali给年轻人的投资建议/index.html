Ali给年轻人的投资建议

Ali给年轻人的投资建议, 句句实用

Inversting your money

  • Do not try to save too much (use the money on cool experiences)
  • Try and open an invesment accounts (for getting to know the process)
  • Invest in index funds
  • Invest in Crypto
  • Invest in learning

Investing your time

  • Build a fastlane mindset
  • Learn useful and in demand skills
  • Document your journey
    • it helps build the craft of writing
    • it is nice to look back upon
    • you might build an audience
  • Learn about video production
  • Become money literate
  • Get a job or work experience
  • Make things for people you admire
    • it helps you develop your own skills
    • it makes learning new things fun
    • it might get you noticed.
  • Try to build a business as a teenager
    • getacoder.com
    • fiverr
    • upwork
    • peopleperhour
  • Try to turn active income into passive income (book: take your shot)
  • Try and dabble with outsourcing
