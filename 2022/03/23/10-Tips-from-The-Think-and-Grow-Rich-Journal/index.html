10 Tips from The Think and Grow Rich Journal

10 Tips from The Think and Grow Rich Journal

  1. Everything you do begins with a thought. and everything you do ultimately defines your destiny.
  2. Purge from your vocabulary words and phrases such as “Can’t”, “maybe”, “I will try my best”, “impossible” and “not likely”, all of which express doubt in the desired outcome.
  3. Embrace adversity and failure as learning opportunities.
  4. Nothing in life is free. what price will you pay to get what you want
  5. When we are not deciding, someone else is deciding for us, and that is no fun, we are not living our lives, others are controlling us.
  6. Locked inside you are the secrets to achieving health, happiness, and self-fulfillment.
  7. A goal without a deadline is merely a wish.
  8. Getting what you want starts with knowing what you want
  9. Your purpose lies within.
  10. The only thing you have absolute control over is what you think.
