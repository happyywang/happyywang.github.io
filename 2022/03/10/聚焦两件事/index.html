聚焦两件事

今日之启示，工作和生活中应该聚焦两件事：

  1. Focusing on what directly produces value.

  2. Focusing on the important and non-urgent thing.

    重要而不紧急的事情包含：

    • planning our career goals

    • building stronger relationships

    • reading books and articles for professional development

    • adopting new productivity and efficiency habits

    • building tools to improve our workflows

    • learning new programming languages etc.
