Chain-smoking的工作方式

Chain-smoking在wiki上的定义是这样的：Chain smoking is the practice of smoking several cigarettes in succession, sometimes using the ember of a finished cigarette to light the next.

Kleon, Austin把Chain-smoking的思想引入到工作中。他建议我们应该用这种方式来工作，并给出具体做法，Here is how you do it: Instead of taking a break in between projects, waiting for feedback, and worrying about what is next, use the end of one project to light up the next one. Just do the work that is in front of you, and when it is finished, ask yourself what you missed, what you could have done better, or what you could not get to, and jump right into the next project.

这可能是全书我最收益的一个建议。因为平时我的行为和Austin提到的似乎完全相反，项目的间隙，我通常会选择休息，放松，什么也不去思考抑或是焦虑的等待。而实际上那正是我们反思的最好时机，继而带着反思之后的或经验或教训快速开启新项目。
