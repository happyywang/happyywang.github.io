凯文凯利的人生建议

凯文凯利KK在68岁生日时给出了68条人生建议，下面四条印象最深刻，记下来，给自己或给孩子们

  1. 练习贫穷，和Stoic的思想不谋而合

    When you are young spend at least 6 months to one year living as poor as you can, owning as little as you possibly can, eating beans and rice in a tiny room or tent, to experience what your worse lifestyle might be. That way any time you have to risk something in the future you won’t be afraid of the worst case scenario.

  2. 有关度假的建议

    On vocation go to the most remote place on your itinerary first, bypassing the cities. You will maximize the shock of otherness in the remote, and then you will welcome the familiar comforts of a city on the way back.

  3. 有关收纳的建议

    Eliminating clutter makes room for your true treasures.

  4. 有关人生路径的建议

    Following your bliss is a recipe for paralysis if you do not know what you are passionate about. A better motto for most youth is “master something, anything“. Through mastery of one thing, you can drift towards extensions of that mastery that bring you more joy, and eventually discover where your bliss is.
