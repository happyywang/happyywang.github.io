David-Perell-50天写作课程（十六）POP写作法

David-Perell-50天写作课程（十六）POP写作法 POP Writing

每一篇文章都可以用三个变量评估它：个性(Personal)，观察性(Observational)，趣味性（Playful）. 我们要找到他们三个之间的平衡点。

Personal writing指文章的故事和情感部分，它由你的经历展开，通过这种情感分享，可以建立你和读者的情感链接。但是太多的personal writing会显得更像一个未经编辑的个人日记，读者会问：我为什么要关心你的这些杂事?

Observational writing指那些被你发现而被别人忽略的点，是你的一些Aha moment。这种敏锐的观察力可以通过3B原则(Bus, Bed, Bath)来获取或者找寻那些看上去不合常理的事情。Adam smth在写他的 不可见的手 一书时充分掌握了观察性写作法则，通过一些轶事和趣味性比喻，让内容易懂易记。但观察行写作少了个人部分和趣味性，纵使内容深刻，也晦涩难懂。

Playful writing指给文章添加一些趣味性。它可以是比喻，可以是思想实验或pop文化。同样要把握度，太多的playful writing会让自己显得像个小丑。Richard Feynmann精通此项平衡树。他在他的物理讲座中，运用比喻和实例点亮了学生的想象力。Tim Urban则是通过有趣的stick figurre drawings向读者科普人工智能和Fermi悖论。

应用POP法则去分析你喜爱的作者的文章，看看他们是如何平衡三者之间的关系的。Then keep the triad in the back of your mind whenever you write.

David-Perell-50天写作课程（十五）寻找你的那枚闪亮硬币

David-Perell-50天写作课程（十五）寻找你的那枚闪亮硬币Find your shiny dime

机场的控制塔对于机场来说就是那枚闪亮的硬币，机场是围绕着控制塔来建造的。

写作也是一样，每篇文章应该先从那枚闪亮的硬币开始，即先确定文章的中心. It represents a tiny but detailed idea that is easy to visualize.

写作也如同健身一样，主题太过宏大会导致无从写起。A shiny dime is the smallest viable idea you can write about. 规则就是，将主题缩减到可以用一句话表达，然后围绕主题开始写作，摒弃和主题无关的任何信息。

Paul Graham说过：Expect 80% of the ideas in an essay to happen after you start writing it.

Every topic is bigger than it seems at first. So instead of writing panoramic thought pieces, focus only on your shiny dime.

David Perell 50天写作课程（十四）:Go For It 窗口

David Perell 50天写作课程（十四）:Go For It 窗口

技术的革新远快于社会规范，两者之间的巨大鸿沟之间充满了机会，但这种机会是有时限的，因此我把它叫做：Go for it window.

当你在清醒和困惑间徘徊，当你有了一些别人没有的idea, 当你知道了一些别人不知道的东西，当别人用黑白的眼光看世界，而你用有色的技术眼睛看世界时，你大概就处在Go for it 窗口。

机会通常和人数成反比的，有时，看起来安全的道路反而最危险。投资人Jim Grant说过：Successful investing is about having people agree with you …. later.

好消息是，发现Go for it window不需要任何隐秘的知识，你只需要自信，并且忽略大众的噪音。

Airbnb就是一个典型的Go for it window的实例，大部分人都觉得租住陌生的房子或出租自己的房子给陌生人都是不可思议的事，但是airbnb开创了新的社会规范，将潜在的需求和未被充分利用的供给相匹配，改变了人们的出行方式，资本化了Go for it window.

Go for it window 是转瞬即逝的(fleeting), when you sense the opportunity, act on it. Research the idea, speak with industry experts and outline a solution.

David Perell 50天写作课程（十三）:用过时的想法横向思考

David Perell 50天写作课程（十三）:用过时的想法横向思考 Lateral thinking with withered ideas

任天堂（Nintendo）有一个开发理念：用过时的技术进行横向思考。

任天堂的工程师创造的好几款爆款游戏都在向人们展示how you can have cutting edge innovation without cutting edge techonogy. 当它的竞争对手们都在试图用很fancy的显示器和先进的计算能力来超越对方时，Nintendo combined readily available technology in new ways.

创新史上这种用过时技术横向思维的例子比比皆是。亨利福特借鉴了2个行业的经验来设计汽车流水线：钟表业更换零部件和罐头业连续流水制造。比特币，21世纪最重要的技术，同样结合了多项技术，有上个世纪70年代的密码学技术，80年代的数字现金研究，90年代的cypherpunk哲学和2000年的数字现金实验。

在写作中，我们可以借鉴任天堂的的设计理念，当全网络都在谈论Netflix的最新节目或者纽约时报的最新畅销书时，你可以pull from old and undervalued ideas instead. Find classic movies through the Criterion Colllection, classic speeches on YouTube and classics that are free to read because they are so old that they no longer have a copyright. Then, write about intellectual phase transitions that emerge.

David Perell 50天写作课程（十二）:寻找那些不合乎常理的事情

David Perell 50天写作课程（十二）:寻找那些不合乎常理的事情 Look for things that do not make sense

亿万富翁投资人Sam Zell每天早上读报纸时，都会留意不那么合乎常理的新闻。有一天，他看到蒙古开了一家星巴克，那里的人不是喝茶的吗，发生了什么事，好奇之下，他飞到蒙古去探个究竟。到了之后，他看到星巴克的分店都开在矿山边上，了解到中国正在经历基础建设热。这让他对中国的经济发展趋势先知先觉。

大部分人对不合常理的事情都选择忽略，Zell则相反，这也某种程度上解释了他投资成功的原因。

对冲基金顾问Adam Robinson曾经说：投资的关键点之一就在于，当你听到脑海里有个声音说it does not make sense，你应该意识到 that is where the gold mine is.

Things that do not make sense are your best learning opportunities. Your confusion is an invitation to recalibrate your module of the world so it better aligns with reality.

如果你在寻找写作ideas，就找那些不合常理的事情吧。在那些不合常理的事情背后，you will find hidden chambers of knowledge that most people are not privy to. the things that surprise you will likely surprise your readers.

David Perell 50天写作课程（十一）:你有自己的风格

David Perell 50天写作课程（十一）:你有自己的风格 You already have a voice

在生命中，你天生就带着特有的behavioral pattens, 你慢慢变大，离家，去成长，这个过程中, you fall, get hurt, get back up and keep going to find you personality。写作也是一样, you should not wait to start writing until you have found your voice.

但是提到写作，人们就会觉得自己没有天赋，自己的语文成绩不够好，又或者觉得自己的想法不够独特，感觉：all my interesting ideas have already been written about.

经济学家Tyler Cowen说：It is the wired that is truly normal. Its how people actually are - what they really care about.

每个人都有自己的独特的声音和个性。但是they are invisible until you socialize. your best readers will be drawn to your voice, so nurture it with unique experiences and a cocktail of obscure influences.

David Perell 50天写作课程（十）:培养创意的3个B

David Perell 50天写作课程（十）:培养创意的3个B

努力思考并不能使你更有创意，相反，Creativity tends to arise in states of relaxation. 虽然创意无法控制，但可以为它创造条件by following the three B’s creativity: bed, bath, bus.

床代表睡眠。闭上眼睛如同重启一个超负荷的电脑，睡眠可以帮你clean the window of your mind，迎来an imaginative day.

洗澡代表放松。Baths are one of the few places in the world where people are not absorbed by the drama of entertainment or the pings of technology, and only once those disappear can they hear themselves think.

Bus代表移动。很多人在走路的时候写作。Morgan Housel说：he walk a lot, several times a day. He thinks so much clearer when he is walking. Its where he write most of his stuff.

由此可见，creativity favors the unfocused and unconcentrated mind. 遵循这个原则，creativity goes from being a mystical talent to a conscious way of being.

David Perell 50天写作课程（九）:像运动员一样学习

David Perell 50天写作课程（九）:像运动员一样学习

LeBron James并非一直是NBA里的最强者，刚入NBA的时候，他的jump shot and field goal percentage并不高。在非赛季，他给自己制定严格的训练计划并在instagram里实时公布自己的训练结果，7年里他的field goal percentage稳步提高，在他的第14个赛季，field goal 已达到54%的骄人成绩。

知识工作者也应该像运动员，音乐家那样制定学习计划，刻意练习。

Nick Maggiulli就是这样的一位学习的典范。2015年他决定学习编程，2年后已经成为数据科学领域的专家。2017年开始他每周二发表一片博文，过去的131周里，从未断更过。

相反，大部分学习者并没有学习计划，他们就像海洋里的冲浪者一样，屈服于环境，跟着浪花起伏。他们的注意力被无止境的邮件，好友推荐和社交媒体所吸引。

我们本可以做的更好。

每一个大项目都可以分解为短期的小任务。Knowing that, you should break down the project into daily increments, and increase a series of daily and weekly goals to learn the skills required to complete the project on time.

The end goal should be clear. Start by writing a positive vision for your future. Focus on the end goal, not the skill itself.

关于制定计划，最好遵循Goldilocks原则：not too easy, not too hard. 如果太难，就停下来继续分解任务。

同时可以在网上分享自己所学。 sharing your ideas will help you digest them, and if your posts are interesting, you may attract experts in you field of curiosity.

By pushing through the cycle of start to finish, you will discover quirks about yourself, accelerate your learning process and ultimately, learn like an athlete.

David Perell 50天写作课程（八）:写作可以节省时间

David Perell 50天写作课程（八）:写作可以节省时间

很多人认为写作需要花费时间，既要打字又要编辑，所以选择不写，短期看是这样，但长期来看写作其实是在帮你节省时间。

将ideas付诸于纸上是一种创建知识资本的方式, because writing is how you make your ideas permanent. 一旦用文字记录下来，你的整个余生都能反复使用它，混编它。

写作的过程是一个研究和开发你的大脑的过程。The words you publish now are the seeds of your future projects. 每当你记录下一个idea，你就为你的知识仓库添加了一个Lego block. 写作如同搭Lego，你的lego块越多，你能搭建的东西就越多。

当你成为一个作家，开启一个项目时，就如同参加一个从中途起跑的马拉松比赛。你已经完成了大部分的工作，只需要从知识仓库中挑选你要写的内容，然后完成它。

The intellectual capital took a long time to build, but it can be reused forever. all those Lego blocks will save you thousands of hours over the course of the career.

面向对象编程里的重要元素解读

面向对象编程里的重要元素解读

OOP is a programming paradigm that relies on the concept of classes and objects.

An object is an instance of a class. Class is a blueprint or template from which objects are created.

An object is a real-world entity such as a pen, mobile, keyboard etc. Class is a group of similar objects

  • 封装概念(Encapsulation)

    Encapsulation refers to the bundling of data, along with the methods that operate on the date, into a single unit.

    类比现实世界里的公司，员工是数据，各个部门是不同的方法。

  • 继承概念Inheritance

    Inheritance is the procedure in which one class called the child class inherits the attributes and methods of another class called the parent class.

  • 抽象概念Abstraction

    Abstraction refers to showing only essential and necessary information to the user and hiding unnecessary information.

    类比手机：无论何种手机，都可打电话，拍照，玩游戏，but It does not show you the inside process of how it is doing things, The implementation parts are hidden.

  • 多态概念(Polymorphism)

    Polymorphism refers to many forms. it is the method that performs different things as per the object’s class which calls it.

    there are 2 types of Polymorphism

    1. Static binding or Compile time polymorphism

      method overloading: refers to a class that can have more than one method of the same name but different parameters.

      (类比现实世界里的人，他可以有多重角色，不同角色做不同的事)

    2. Dynamic binding or runtime polymorphism

      method overriding: allows a subclass or child class to provide a specific implementation of a method that is already provided by one of its super-classes or parent classes.
